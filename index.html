About

I am a multidisciplinary Data Analyst with a strong track record of delivering actionable insights across healthcare, market research, and business intelligence. My expertise spans SQL, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI, enabling me to design robust data models and develop interactive dashboards that transform complex information into clear, strategic direction.

My professional journey bridges the precision of data analytics with the human-centered focus of healthcare and human resource management. I have worked on projects that not only informed high-level decision-making but also contributed to tangible improvements in operational efficiency, patient care, and stakeholder engagement.

Beyond technical execution, I am driven by a guiding principle: data with purpose. Every dataset tells a story, and I excel in uncovering the narratives that matter most—identifying trends, predicting outcomes, and streamlining reporting processes to empower leadership teams.
